About Us

Know about us

ImangaTech is an information technological and business consultation company that is based in Ndola Copperbelt province of Zambia. It was founded in the year 2018 and the motivation to its existence is to provide quality IT and business related solutions to our target audience and customers.

Our services are categorized into six major components which are business consultation, Company Registration, IT consultation, website design and development, Graphics Design, copy writing and digital marketing. Our focus is to help businesses grow and provide innovative solutions to modern day problems.
